------------------------------------input file----------------------------------

0 -> Id of the order placed
1 -> Area where the order was delivered
2 -> Name of the product
3 -> Quantity of the product delivered in that order
4 -> Brand of the ordered product

------------------------------------output file 1----------------------------------

0 -> product Name.
1 -> average quantity of the product purchased per order. (average is the quantity sold divided by total orders)


------------------------------------output file 2----------------------------------


0 -> product Name.
1 -> popular brand. (the brand with the most total orders for the item)
